What is aQuila Software?

Our application, AquilaTrack is one of the most used track-n-trace portals in India with more than 35,000 registered devices. Our platform currently hosts tailor-made solutions for Fleet Management, Cabs & Taxi Aggregators, Self-Drive/Rental services, Emergency Medical Services (Ambulance),Logistics Transports and School Transport Management. Our team comprises highly skilled individuals with an extensive range of capabilities. All our solutions are hosted on a cloud architecture and we duly follow the agile methodology for software development. Our big data platform is integrated with advanced business intelligence and analytics and we also provide in-house hardware expertise.
