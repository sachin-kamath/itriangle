AIS140 Solution

What is AIS140?

Automotive Industry Standard 140 (AIS 140) is a set of standards directed by MoRTH & published by ARAI (Automotive) for vehicle tracking system and emergency request button. The government has directed every state’s public transport department to make sure that all passenger carrying buses conform to the AIS 140 guidelines by 1 st April 2018 later the implementation was postponed to 01 st January 2019 . In order to comply with AIS 140 guidelines, every passenger, National Permit Commercial vehicles needs to have a GPS tracking system and emergency button. This will help the department in tracking the Vehicle in case of any emergency and also enable the passengers to apprise the control room of any kind of mishap or other emergencies. AIS 140 standard will also play a vital role in bringing the much-needed change in transport industry. Incorporation of Advance Driver Assistance System (ADAS) and Augmented Reality (AR) in public transport vehicles will be expedited once this standard is implemented in its entirety.

Adavantages of AIS140 Solution

1. Real time tracking of the passenger vehicles by the govt agency and the customer.
2. AIS 140 compliant hardware/software would help relevant authorities gain control, enhance efficiency, quality, comfort and safety. The system is supposed to optimize transportation across various states by utilizing existing transport infrastructure and mitigating the challenges in road traffic management.
3. With the help of historical data, such as animated replay of trips, alerts, exceptions and meaningful reports, authorities can investigate such incidents and take appropriate measures to curb previously unknown/unidentified risks involved in operations.
4. Faster responses to unforeseen incidents, vehicle break-down or an accident. In short, every event requires immediate attention.
5. Transmission of data to three IP addresses simultaneously, out of which one is reserved for SOS button.
