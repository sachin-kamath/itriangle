Connected Mobility Solution

What is Connected Mobility Solution?

The Connected Mobility Solution has been packaged keeping in mind the requirements of both the automobile manufacturer and the end user. Based on the scope and the area of applicability (like 2 wheelers, 3 wheelers, 4 wheelers and EV segment), the ACMA can be customised. Below listed are the offerings.

  • End User Mobile application
    • Location Services
    • Performance Analytics
    • Geoservices - Security (anti-theft based on location), Trip and Routing Management
    • Alerts
    • Maintenance
    • Roadside Assistance
    • Social Connectivity
  • Device Management Portal
    • API Solution (for integration with other tools and softwares)
    • SIM and Data Management
    • Device Inventory Management
    • User profile Management
    • Reporting and Analytics
    • Firmware Over the Air (FOTA) tool
    • End of Line (EOL) tool
