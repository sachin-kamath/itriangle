aQuila School Solution

The world is moving towards Industry 4.0, but parents even today are anxious about their child reaching school and back home. We at Zeliot address this concern, the concern of the hour! Our Aquila School Solution has been designed keeping in forefront the requirement of both the parents and the school management. Apart from providing live tracking of the child to the parents, the focus is also on increasing the efficiency and reducing costs on the fleet.

We offer the AquilaSchool web application to the school admin and mobile application to the parents.To address the challenges of school logistics, our with solution comes with,

  • Tracking Hardware (device to be fitted in the school bus)
  • Tracking Software (Web portal and mobile app)
  • Accessories such as RFID cards

Our solution can also be integrated with any existing ERP. This provides a seamless experience to the school administration and parents through a mobile application.

Advantages Of Aquila School Solution:

  • The school management is benefited by,
    • Knowing the count of students who would be boarding the bus prior to the start of pick-up. This enables the admin to plan the optimal pick-up and drop routes
    • Marking (geofencing) the waypoints (pick-up and drop points) to ensure the buses do not miss any point along the route.
    • Adding or merging of buses if overlapping along the same routes.
    • Easing attendance management, thus avoiding the time consumed for manual attendance inside the classroom
    • Enabling optimal usage of the bus fleet
    • Knowing the driving performance and driver behaviour by capturing events such as overspeeding, harsh acceleration, harsh braking and emergency. These events are shown as alerts in real time thus enabling the admin to enhance the safety of the students.
    • Providing reports for each trip wise, day wise and customer time range to make a detailed analysis.

aQuilaTrack School Mobile Application

The parent app on the mobile enables each parent with,

  • Knowing the attendance of their child through RFID integration for boarding and deboarding the bus.
  • Easy access to live tracking of their child.
  • Estimated Time of Arrival (ETAs) for pick-up and drop points.
  • Real time alerts in case of emergencies such as bus breakdown and accident.
  • Marking their child as absent in case of pre-planned leave. This helps the admin to plan the pick-up and drop routes efficiently.
  • Managing multiple children through the same login with unique student IDs.
