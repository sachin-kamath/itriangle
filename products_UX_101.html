4G Telematics Device

UX 101

UX101 is AIS140 Certified 4G tracking device . UX 101 can be described as a High end Compact Vehicle tracking/Fleet management black box with rugged IP67 casing. Our premium 4G enabled device comes with lots of connectivity options, Speed Processing power Its ability to tightly integrate with CAN bus of the Vehicle, enables to implement various connected Vehicle use cases. It has optional hotspot & Bluetooth features. It can be used as a fleet management device in various domains such as Logistic, taxi operation, car/bike rentals, security tracking, insurance telematics, asset tracking, etc. integrated with internal antennae which makes it suitable for hidden/stealth installations.
